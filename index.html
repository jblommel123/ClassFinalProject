Eclipse Racing Team

We are a group of racing nerds that enjoy competing in races online and sharing a love of motorsport in the virtual world.

Welcome to our website. We hope you'll have a look around and hopefully we will see you out on track.

The games we play are not like mario kart or need for speed. These are sims that aim to create a realistic experience so that you feel like a racing driver.
