Games

Gran Turismo Sport

Gran Turismo Sport is the latest entry into Sony's franchise that is exclusive to PlayStation. It is known for being slightly more arcade like but was featured at the summer olympics.

Assetto Corsa Competizione

ACC is the official game of the SRO's GT World Challenge series that is considered the gold standard in the sim racing world of GT3.

David Perel called this game the closest a simulation can come to like driving a GT3 car in real life.

iRacing

The 800 pound gorilla of the sim racing world. They have the most complete one stop shop ecosystem when it comes to being able to hop online and race.
